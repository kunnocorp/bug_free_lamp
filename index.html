Features

Seamless Data Sharing

Data sharing is at our core. Get your information in front of the people that matter without repeating data entry or paying for multiple posting sites. You can if you want to, but now you don’t have to. Kunno is a productivity tool that will help you spend your time in more meaningful ways.

Privacy Settings

You control your message and your audience. Every listing you add has privacy settings. Choose to make your information available to the public, to realtors only, or private. This is a fantastic strategic feature for REIT’s, exclusive listings, new development launches and industry previews.

Social Marketing

Getting your message out is all part of our plan. So, of course Kunno is designed to connect to your social media. Add your accounts and adjust the settings to your liking to share your blog, new listings and promote your brand.

Productivity Tools

Helping real estate professionals build a better business online is our mission. Our talented developers are hard at work creating amazing features that will help you become more productive and more profitable. Join the Kunno community and let’s build great businesses together.

Themes

Control your brand and your message. Make Kunno your primary web presence or your supplementary marketing tool. It’s entirely up to you. Connect all of your social media accounts, input all of your company contacts, and showcase your sales representatives on each listing. Use your own domain, or enjoy a www.kunno.co/yourbusiness extension.


Templates

Choose a beautiful mobile friendly template from our theme store. Create a custom theme with a developer of your choice, or work with our talented designers to find the solutions you’re looking for. Change your look as often as you like. .

Web Hook

Continue using your existing site and benefit from the Kunno property search tool. Simply have your web developer integrate our Web Hook into your theme. In no time you’ll be promoting your properties in any market, anywhere.

Exclusive Membership

All buyers, tenants and real estate professionals looking for commercial or residential properties for sale or lease are invited to use Kunno to find their next property or business. Searching is free and always will be.

Kunno listing and marketing services are free, but only available exclusively to real estate professionals. Membership is available to landlords, developers, property managers, agents and brokerage firms. Sign up and let us know which professional associations you’re affiliated with, licenses you hold or explain why you feel you qualify. Our verification team will quickly review your application. Kunno is inclusive of the entire professional real estate industry and not intended to be used as a for sale by owner platform.
